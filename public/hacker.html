Identity yourself, Hacker

In Pwn2K you play as a computer hacker in a fictionalised version of the Internet based upon a mixture of contemporary and historical technology and culture. Competing against - and sometimes cooperating with - your friends, you'll aim to amass cred: a mark of your worth and influence as a hacker.

The first thing you need is your hacker name. It can be your real name, if you're of a courageous disposition, but "Neo" is infinitely cooler than "Thomas Anderson". You can even use l33tsp33k, if that's the kind of h4x0r you are. Choose wisely, and don't be a lamer.
